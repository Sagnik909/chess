About the Tournament

There will be 20 contestants partipating in this 1 month long Chess Championship. The 20 contestants will be divided into 10 pairs. Each pair will play against each other. The winners will play with other winners and so on till the final(Date to be decided). The names of the top five contestants will appear in the website's "2022 Leaders" section. The game will be a game of chess. The time of the play will be the tiffin time of the school. The contestants will be notified on the match day. If the contestant is absent or not present at the time of play, he will be marked as a loser. A gift box will be given at the end of the tournament to the champion and the semi-finalists will also be given prizes. A fairplay award will also be given to the contestant who has been most fair.

About the Game

The game will be a classic game of chess. There will be no fixed time limit. If the game is not finished untill the end of tiffin time, the winner will be decided by seeing how many pieces each contestant has captured and calculating the points. The points for the pieces are as follows:-

Queen: 9 points

Rook: 6 points

Knight: 5 points

Bishop: 4 points

Pawn: 2 points
